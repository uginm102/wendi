Transfer Money from Wendi to a Bank Account

The most convenient method to make a bank transfer is via the Wendi mobile app. Follow the steps below to complete your transfer quickly and securely.

1

Welcome to Wendi

Open your Wendi app for Quick access to Pay Bills, Airtime/Bundles, School Fees, and Wendi QR Pay.

2

Secure Login

Enter your 4-digit PIN to access your Wendi account.

3

Home Dashboard

Manage your money with ease — Deposit, Send, Withdraw, Savings, Loans, SACCOs, Airtime, and more.

4

Financial Services

Move your money your way — Banking, Other Wallets, Western Union, Merchant Payments, and Standing Orders.

5

Choose Your Bank

Select from supported banks, including Stanbic Bank, Pearl Bank, GTBank, Pride Bank, and others.

6

Wendi to Bank

Choose the service you need — seamlessly transfer money from your Wendi wallet directly to any bank account.

7

Deposit to Bank

Enter the bank account number and amount you wish to transfer.

8

Confirm Transaction

Review details before sending.

9

Transaction Feedback

Clear feedback when a transaction is executed (e.g., insufficient balance).

Transfers are typically processed instantly. If your transfer is delayed beyond 30 minutes, please contact Wendi support.
