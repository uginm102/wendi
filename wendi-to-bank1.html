Transfer Money from Wendi to a Bank Account

The most convenient method to make a bank transfer is via the Wendi mobile app. Follow the steps below to complete your transfer quickly and securely.

Replace placeholders with real app screenshots

1

Log in to Wendi

Open the Wendi mobile app and log in using your Touch ID, Face ID, or PIN.

2

Open Financial Services

From the Home menu, tap Financial Services.

3

Select Banking

Tap on Banking to see available bank transfer options.

4

Choose your destination bank

Select the bank you wish to transfer funds to, then tap Wendi to Bank.

5

Enter payee details

Enter the payee's account number and the amount you wish to transfer. Tap Next to proceed.

6

Confirm the transfer

Review the payee details carefully, then tap Confirm to complete the transfer.

Transfers are typically processed instantly. If your transfer is delayed beyond 30 minutes, please contact Wendi support.
